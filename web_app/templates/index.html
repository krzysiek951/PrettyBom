Features

PrettyBom Features

As a result of processing your .csv file, you will receive a Bill of Materials in .xlsx (Excel) format. Learn what functionalities your BOM gains.

Part sets and quantity to order detection

Our tool will go through every BOM position to tell you how many parts you need to order.

Part type detection

Our tool will automatically detect your parts as: production, purchased, fastener or junk.

Advanced sorting

Your Bill of Materials will be neat and clean by sorting alphabetically and organizing parts in the following order: production, purchased, fastener, junk.

Part and assembly detection

You will filter your Bill of Materials with ease with additional fields: part and assembly.

Name normalization

Your Bill of Materials probably includes a part supplier where the name might be Elesa Ganter or Elesa-Ganter. The name normalization function will allow you to easily unify that names.

Parent assembly detection

Your Bill of Materials can now be filtered by parent assembly, so you can easily check if everything you need to order for any assembly is included.

Check if you have discovered all the Features

  • A new property "Parent assembly"
    Now you can easily filter your BOM by parent assembly to get BOM for every assembly. Main assembly, which is the one, from which Bill of Materials was exported is M-2022-00 Layout, quantity 1.
  • A new property "File type"
    File type can be set as assembly or part. This gives you the ability to easily filter your BOM to get only production parts.
  • A new property "Type"
    Type can be set as production, purchased, fasteners and junks. In our example production parts were detected by the "M-2022" keyword.
  • A new property "Sets"
    Sets gives you the information how many sets of given part need to be ordered.
  • A new property "To order"
    To order lets you know the total parts number needed to be ordered.
  • Supplier name unification
    Originally supplier column had different names for same supplier like Elesa-Ganter and ELESA GANTER. Now everything has been unified.
  • Advanced sorting
    Whole Bill of Material was sorted with following order: production, purchased, fastener, junks. PrettyBom also takes into account the nesting of the parts!.
